Best Time to Visit

Dry Season

June - September & December - February

  • Best for wildlife viewing
  • Easy road access to parks
  • Gorilla trekking at its best
  • Peak tourist season

Wet Season

March - May & October - November

  • Lush green landscapes
  • Bird watching is excellent
  • Fewer tourists
  • Lower accommodation rates

Shoulder Seasons

May-June & October-November

  • Balance of good weather and fewer crowds
  • Good value for money
  • Migratory birds present
  • Variable weather conditions

Conservation Efforts

Protecting Uganda's Natural Heritage

Uganda's national parks play a crucial role in conservation:

  • Mountain Gorilla Conservation: Bwindi and Mgahinga parks protect over half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas
  • Community Involvement: 20% of park entrance fees support local community projects
  • Anti-Poaching Units: Dedicated ranger teams protect wildlife from illegal activities
  • Habitat Restoration: Reforestation programs in degraded areas of the parks
