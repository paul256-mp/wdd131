Uganda Landscape

About Uganda

Uganda, known as the "Pearl of Africa," is a landlocked country in East Africa renowned for its diverse landscapes, abundant wildlife, and warm hospitality. From the source of the Nile River to the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains, Uganda offers breathtaking natural beauty.

The country is home to over half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas, making it one of the premier destinations for primate tracking and eco-tourism in Africa.

Top Attractions

Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

Home to nearly half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas, offering unforgettable gorilla trekking experiences.

Murchison Falls

Where the Nile River forces itself through a narrow gorge, creating one of Africa's most powerful waterfalls.

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Famous for tree-climbing lions and diverse ecosystems including savannah, forests, and crater lakes.
